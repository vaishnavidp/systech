Our Services

Comprehensive support for your German education journey

Career Counseling & University Selection

Expert guidance in choosing the right course and university based on your academic background, interests, and career goals. We help you make informed decisions about your educational path in Germany.

  • Personalized career assessment
  • University shortlisting based on your profile
  • Course selection guidance
  • Admission requirements analysis

APS Certification Assistance

Complete support for APS certification process, which is mandatory for Indian students applying to German universities. We guide you through document preparation and submission.

  • Document preparation guidance
  • Application form filling assistance
  • Interview preparation
  • Follow-up with APS office

Application Support

Comprehensive assistance with university applications, including SOPs, LORs, and CV preparation. We ensure your application stands out and meets all requirements.

  • Statement of Purpose (SOP) writing
  • Letter of Recommendation (LOR) guidance
  • CV/Resume preparation
  • Application form filling

Visa Filing & Mock Interviews

Expert guidance for student visa application process, including document preparation, application submission, and interview preparation to maximize your chances of success.

  • Document checklist preparation
  • Application form filling
  • Mock visa interviews
  • Interview preparation guidance

German Language Coaching

Comprehensive German language training from A1 to B2 level, preparing you for both academic requirements and daily life in Germany. Our experienced language trainers ensure you achieve the required proficiency.

  • Structured language courses
  • Test preparation (TestDaF, DSH)
  • Speaking practice sessions
  • Cultural orientation

Blocked Account & Health Insurance

Assistance with financial requirements for studying in Germany, including setting up a blocked account and arranging health insurance coverage as per visa requirements.

  • Blocked account setup guidance
  • Health insurance arrangement
  • Financial planning assistance
  • Document preparation

Accommodation & Part-Time Jobs

Support in finding suitable accommodation in Germany and guidance on part-time job opportunities to help you manage your expenses while studying.

  • Accommodation search assistance
  • Housing application support
  • Part-time job guidance
  • Work permit information

Pre-Departure Orientation

Comprehensive pre-departure guidance to prepare you for life in Germany, including cultural orientation, packing tips, and important information about student life.

  • Cultural orientation sessions
  • Packing checklist
  • Travel arrangements guidance
  • Student life information
